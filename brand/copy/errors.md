# Error Pages & States

## 404 — Page Not Found

**Headline:** This page doesn't exist.
**Body:** Either it never did, or it was destroyed. Both are intentional.
**CTA:** Go to dashboard

---

## 500 — Server Error

**Headline:** Something broke.
**Body:** Not your fault. Not your problem. We're already looking at it. Come back in a few minutes.
**CTA:** Try again

---

## 503 — Maintenance

**Headline:** We're recalibrating.
**Body:** Scheduled maintenance. Your subscriptions are unaffected. The dashboard will be back shortly.

---

## Permission Denied (403)

**Headline:** You don't have access to this.
**Body:** If you think that's wrong, check that you're logged into the right account.
**CTA:** Go to dashboard

---

## Session Expired (401)

**Headline:** Your session ended.
**Body:** You were gone too long. Sign in again.
**CTA:** Sign in

---

## Payment Failed

**Headline:** Payment didn't go through.
**Body:** The Lightning invoice expired or the payment failed. Your membership is unchanged for now — try again before your billing date.
**CTA:** Retry payment

---

## Payment Failed — Membership at Risk

**Headline:** Still unpaid.
**Body:** Your membership expires in [N] days. If we don't receive payment by [date], your credentials and all stored data will be destroyed. This is not negotiable.
**CTA:** Pay now

---

## Empty State — No Services Added

**Headline:** Nothing here yet.
**Body:** Add at least two streaming services to get your rotation started.
**CTA:** Add services

---

## Empty State — Queue Empty After Removal

**Headline:** Your queue is empty.
**Body:** You removed all your services. Add some back to resume rotation.
**CTA:** Add services

---

## Empty State — No Payment History

**Body:** No payments yet.

---

## Empty State — No Rotation History

**Body:** No rotations completed yet. Your first one is coming.

---

## Rate Limited (429)

**Headline:** Slow down.
**Body:** Too many requests. Wait a moment and try again.

---

## Invite Code Invalid

**Headline:** That code doesn't work.
**Body:** It's either expired, already used, or never existed. If someone gave it to you, ask them for a new one.

---

## Invite Code Required

**Headline:** You need an invite.
**Body:** UnsaltedButter is invite-only. If you know someone who's a member, ask them. If you don't, get on the waitlist.
**CTA:** Join waitlist

---

## Account Destroyed (post-expiry login attempt)

**Headline:** This account no longer exists.
**Body:** Your membership expired and all associated data was destroyed. There is nothing to recover. If you want back in, you'll need a new invite.
**CTA:** Join waitlist
